What Is Dancing ?

The activity of dancing for pleasure or in order to entertain others.

Dancing represents, happiness, contentment, victory, confidence and also celebration.
Dance is more than the exploring of different ways to make a shape or learning a series of steps to music; it is a way of moving that uses the body as an instrument of expression and communication.

Dance is a performing art form consisting of purposefully selected sequences of human movement. This movement has aesthetic and symbolic value, and is acknowledged as dance by performers and observers within a particular culture.
